# Cactus landing page direction

## Brand story

This is a single-page website for a local, family-owned business that grows and sells cacti.

The page should communicate:

- family warmth
- care and craft
- regional authenticity
- polished product presentation
- confidence without losing humility

## Visual direction

- Editorial landing page rather than marketplace grid
- Organic collage-style image arrangements
- Soft cream background with deep green anchors
- Terracotta used as a small, warm accent
- Rounded containers, curved separators, and layered cards

## Content priorities

1. Immediate emotional hero with family/business context
2. Clear reasons to trust the growers
3. Product highlights or signature cactus varieties
4. Packaging, delivery, or greenhouse-care credibility
5. A strong contact/visit CTA for a local business

## Tone of voice

- Warm
- grounded
- concise
- knowledgeable
- never salesy or loud

## Implementation notes

- Reuse the existing cactus and grower imagery already in `public/images`
- Prefer real business-style copy over generic startup language
- Keep each section visually distinct while still part of one system
- Build a homepage that can plausibly reflect a photo-led design reference
